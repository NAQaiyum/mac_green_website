MacGreen Agrocomm Ltd., established in 2023 in Raghabpur-Shambhuganj, Bangladesh, is a modern agricultural business on 223 decimals of land. Specializing in sustainable agriculture, our focus includes vegetables, fruits, crops, livestock farming, production, and trading. Committed to ethical practices, we aim to enhance the agro-industry by diversifying our business into various segments.
